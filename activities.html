Activities

Downtown Asheville invites you to stroll through its lively streets, where a captivating mix of eclectic boutiques, vibrant art galleries, and a diverse culinary scene beckon. Immerse yourself in the city's artistic pulse by visiting the River Arts District, where local artists open their studios, providing a behind-the-scenes glimpse into their creative processes.

Explore the River Arts District, a thriving community where artistic expression thrives, to uncover the essence of Asheville's artistic character. Wander around galleries and studios and watch as the local artists transform their ideas into reality. Talk to the artists and maybe even purchase a one-of-a-kind work of art to bring home, making your visit a special and unforgettable one. Asheville's dedication to preserving its cultural identity is demonstrated by the River Arts District, a vibrant area where the arts flourish.

Things To Do!

As the sun sets, downtown Asheville comes alive with a symphony of flavors. Dive into the city's culinary scene by embarking on a food tour, where you can sample a diverse array of dishes crafted by talented local chefs.
