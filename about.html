About The Logde

Asheville, North Carolina, is tucked away in the picturesque embrace of the Blue Ridge Mountains and beckons with a tapestry of activities that draw tourists from near and far. Situated at the center of this cultural sanctuary is the magnificent Biltmore Estate, a stately home encircled by expansive gardens and a well-known winery.

We are thrilled to announce the grand opening of our brand-new bed and breakfast, where comfort and hospitality converge to create an unforgettable experience for our guests. Nestled in a charming location, we are eager to open our doors and welcome visitors to a haven of relaxation and warmth.
